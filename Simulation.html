Plaque Heart Simulation

« created with Unity »

How to Work:

  • To rotate use the arrow keys or the WASD keys
  • To move forward use the space bar
  • To move backwards use the backspace key or delete key
  • To stop moving backwards or forwards press the fullstop button
  • To turn the plaque on and off press the p key
  • To change the camera view press the c key
